Reading seminar: Freedman's proof of the 4-dimensional Poincaré conjecture

Wintersemester 2021/2022

Wednesdays, 13:15, seminar room 1.315


Content: The goal of this reading seminar is to understand Freedman's proof of the 4-dimensional Poincaré conjecture.
We will read every week a chapter from [BKKPR] and discuss the content in the Wednesdays slots.
